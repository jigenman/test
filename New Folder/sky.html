SKY PROCYCLING


Team Sky (UCI team code: SKY) is a British professional cycling team that competes in the UCI World Tour. The team is based at the National Cycling Centre in Manchester, England, with a logistics base in Belgium and an operational base in Quarrata, Italy.[1] The team is managed by British Cycling's performance director Dave Brailsford.
